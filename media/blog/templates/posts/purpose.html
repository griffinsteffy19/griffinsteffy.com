The Idea Behind It

If there is one thing you should know about me is that I am not a confident writer. As I wrote papers during school, I tended to proctrastinate (maybe like yourself). However, I rarely waited till the last minute due to a lack of an idea. Sometimes it was no motivation, and seeing that I became an engineer, I bet you could have figured that one out. That being said, I do believe the larger blocker for me was my confidence in what I put on paper. I had (and still do have) this notion in my mind that I am not capable of writing something worth sharing on paper. Thus this blog is born... I don't have a specific interest in improving my writing, but essentially I have read some things online that improving writing can improve knowledge, plus I need an outlet for my thoughts, and this seems like a private place to start.

What can you expect from me?

My goals for this is to publish 52 times in the next 52 weeks. Ideally this looks like a weekly upload, but I'm starting with a low bar that I can consistently jump over, even when I lack motivation. There are also no constraints on content. One week you might get an insightful writing piece, and the next I'll talk about a cool programming accomplishment I made, who knows what is in store for all of us. the main goal is for this platform serve as a way for me to let me creative juices flow, but give motivation to be creative on an average of a weekly basis. The worst thing that can happen to a creative is that they stop creating. Let's not let that happen here.
